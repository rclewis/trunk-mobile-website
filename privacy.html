Privacy Policy

Effective July 22, 2026

Trunk: Smart Packing Assistant (“Trunk”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.


1. Information We Collect

Trunk has no user accounts, and we do not build a profile of you. Your trips, items, packing lists, bags, and photos are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to us.

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your device’s advertising identifier
  • Your GPS or background location — Trunk never requests location permission

There are two exceptions to “stays on your device,” both about weather, and together they cover everything Trunk itself sends off your device. When you search for a destination, what you type is sent to a location lookup service so it can suggest matching places. When we show a forecast, the destination’s coordinates and the trip’s date range are sent to a weather service. Both are described in section 2. Device backups are a separate matter, covered in section 5.

2. Weather and Third-Party Services

Trunk uses these services. We send none of them your name, email, or any account identifier — Trunk has none to send.

  • WeatherAPI — as you type a destination while planning a trip, what you’ve typed is sent to WeatherAPI so it can suggest matching places and turn your choice into structured location data (city, region, coordinates). Suggestions begin after two characters, so partial text is sent as you type. Nothing else is sent — no GPS, no background location.
  • Apple WeatherKit (iPhone and iPad) — to fetch the forecast for a trip, your destination’s coordinates and the trip’s start and end dates are sent to Apple. Apple’s handling of that request is governed by Apple’s own privacy policy.
  • Our weather service (Android) — Apple WeatherKit is not available on Android, so on Android the same request (destination coordinates and trip dates) goes to a small service we operate on Amazon Web Services, which forwards it to Apple’s weather data and returns the forecast. This request is not tied to any account or persistent identifier: the app obtains temporary, anonymous AWS credentials that are discarded when the app closes. We do not use these requests to build a profile. The coordinates are used only to fetch that forecast and are not stored. We do not log your IP address on this service; our operational logs contain only error diagnostics and request identifiers, and are automatically deleted after 14 days.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — if you tap “Rate Trunk”, the store’s own review prompt is shown. We receive no information from it, and we never see what you rate or write.

We do not share any personal information with any of these services, and we do not sell data to anyone.

3. Photos and Camera

You can optionally attach a photo to an item, either by taking one or choosing one from your photo library. Trunk asks for camera or photo access only at the moment you use that feature, and you can decline.

Photos you attach are copied into Trunk’s own storage on your device. They are never uploaded to us or to any third party, and Trunk does not read your photo library beyond the specific image you select.

4. In-App Purchases

Trunk may in the future offer in-app purchases (for example, a Pro subscription) through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. All payment processing is handled by those platforms. We do not collect or store payment information ourselves.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your app data is stored locally on your device using the operating system’s standard app storage. You are responsible for backing up your device.

If you delete the app, the application data stored on your device — including your trips, items, packing lists, and any photos you attached — is removed with it.

If you have device backups turned on, your Trunk data is included in them, the same as other apps’ data. That is deliberate: Trunk has no accounts and no cloud sync, so a device backup is what stands between you and losing your trips and photos if your phone is lost, damaged, or replaced. Those backups are held by Apple or Google under their own policies rather than by us, which also means a copy in a backup is not removed by deleting the app — removing it follows Apple’s or Google’s process.

Requests to weather services are sent over encrypted connections (HTTPS).

6. Deleting Your Data

Because your data stays on your device and we keep no copy on our servers, there is no deletion request to make of us — we hold nothing to delete.

  • Individual entries — you can delete a trip, item, bag, saved packing list, or an attached photo inside the app at any time. Deleting a photo removes Trunk’s copy from your device; if you chose that photo from your photo library, the original there is not affected.
  • Everything at once — delete the app. That removes the application data stored on your device, subject to the device-backup note in section 5.
  • Weather requests — nothing to delete. Destination coordinates are used only to fetch a forecast and are not stored, and our operational logs, which do not contain your IP address, are automatically deleted after 14 days.

If you have a question about any of this, contact us at the address in section 9.

7. Children’s Privacy

Trunk is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage users to review this page periodically for any updates. Continued use of the app after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

If we introduce features that materially affect how your data is handled — such as cloud storage or user accounts — we will provide additional notice and may require you to accept the updated terms before continuing to use those features.
